Why Mount Abu is known as hill station?

Mount Abu is known as a hill station because it's a cool, elevated retreat in the Aravalli Range, offering relief from Rajasthan's desert heat with lush greenery, Nakki Lake, and a distinct cool climate, making it an oasis and the state's only hill station

About Dilwara Temple(A Temple which is located in mount abu)

Dilwara Temples - The Dilwara Temples were built between the 11th and 13th centuries under the Solanki dynasty, with the first temple, Vimal Vasahi, completed in 1031 AD by Vimal Shah
